I wanted a place that was truly mine: no algorithms in between, no templates, no vanity metrics. Just what I think, what I build, and what makes me curious.

## Decisions

- **Bilingual at the root.** Every page exists in Spanish and English, with a switcher that respects where you are.
- **"Ink Minimal" theme.** Paper and ink, Space Grotesk type, all monochrome except one terracotta accent.
- **The ink mascot.** A line-being that blinks, follows you with its gaze, and reacts to scroll.

## Ongoing

It keeps growing. Every time I learn something, this site notices first.
